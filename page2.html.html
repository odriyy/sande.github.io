of Autonomous Robots aims to create a machine that can clean

Groups

To ease workload, the team is divided into three groups

Mechanics

Electronics

Softwares

The mechanics group is responsible for handling the structural parts of the robot, ensuring its stability and functionality. This includes tasks such as redesigning the chassis,trash collection system, and the mobility of the robot. Additionally, this group works on the design of the robot to ensure it meets ergonomic and functional needs while optimizing weight distribution and material use for durability.This group also handles prototyping to testing.

This project was taken up mainly to reduce the physical strain
experienced by our cleaners everyday, by automating the process.
Since SAND-E is still in its prototyping stage, it is not able to
fully take over the roles and responsibilities of the cleaner but
in due time, it will be able to clean the beach autonomously,
without the need for arduous labour.
